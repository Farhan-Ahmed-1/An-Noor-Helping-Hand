Funeral Assistance

A service that aims to help grieving families through the difficult process of burying their loved ones.

An-Noor understands the difficulties that come with the passing of a family member. This is why An-Noor aims to help the family through every step of the process, from washing and wrapping the body to burying the body all according to Islamic legislation. We also try to provide as much emotional and financial support as we can.

Emotional Support

Compassionate guidance and support during difficult times

Islamic Procedures

Complete assistance with Islamic funeral rites and traditions

Financial Aid

Support for families facing financial hardship

Community Support

Connection with community resources and support networks
