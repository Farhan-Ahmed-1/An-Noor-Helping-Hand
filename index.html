Why Choose An-Noor?

Dedicated to Serving Humanity

  • We provide comprehensive support to families in need through food assistance, education programs, and emergency aid, ensuring no one in our community is left behind.

  • We maintain complete transparency in our operations and fund allocation, ensuring your donations reach those who need them most effectively and efficiently.

  • Our programs focus on creating lasting change through education, skill development, and community empowerment initiatives that help families become self-reliant.

Our Impact

We provide essential support to disadvantaged communities, ensuring a brighter future for those in need.

At An-Noor Helping Hand, we are dedicated to improving lives by addressing fundamental needs such as food, water, healthcare, and education. Our initiatives empower individuals and families, fostering self-reliance and long-term growth.

  • Providing vital aid through Ramadan food drives, Qurbani distribution, and emergency assistance to thousands of families
  • Supporting education through Back to School Programs and Orphan Care Centers
  • Implementing sustainable projects like Tube Wells and reforestation efforts
  • Transforming over 23,000 lives through compassionate action and community support
